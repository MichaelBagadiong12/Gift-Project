My Letter

Dear Love,

Each day has been wonderful ever since you came to my life. Not being able to talk to you feels like my day is incomplete. Meeting you was such an unexpected blessing to me, I have prayed for times that I will meet someone who will love me like the way I do. Now that I found you, I’ve realized that I can still trust and love someone again. If I have to describe you, you were like a hidden gem to me, rare and one of a kind. Before meeting you, I found peace in my own company. Love can hurt you to the point that you’d prefer taking your path alone rather than having someone that causes you chaos. Though my perception of love was changed when I found you. There are times when I feel lost and down but somehow, I end my day with a smile, your existence and voice are enough to make me happy. Being with you feels like I can achieve everything and surpass any challenges.

Every moment I spend with you feels like something I never want to lose. You’ve become such an important part of my life, and I find myself appreciating even the smallest things because they remind me of you. The way you make me feel is something I can’t easily put into words, but I know it’s real and something I want to hold on to. Words aren’t enough to say that I really love you, so I’d like to ask for your permission…

Can I court you and love you with all of my heart?
